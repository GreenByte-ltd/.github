# GreenByte


We build software for the flower industry — covering the full supply chain from farms and distribution centers to flower shops and last-mile delivery.

Our technology helps floral businesses manage inventory, orders, logistics, freshness, fulfillment, and customer experience through purpose-built digital infrastructure.

> **Delivering timeless elegance in every bouquet.**

We invest in technology to make the flower business more efficient, transparent, and scalable — while preserving the beauty and quality behind every floral product.
